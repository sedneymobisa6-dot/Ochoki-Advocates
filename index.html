The Firm

Built on trust & expertise

Founded in Kisii, Ben K. Gichana & Company Advocates has grown from a boutique practice into one of Nyanza Province's most trusted legal institutions. Under the founding leadership of Mr. Benard Kiriama Gichana, the firm established a reputation defined by analytical precision and strategic acumen.

Our attorneys are admitted Advocates of the High Court of Kenya, with extensive experience across both contentious and non-contentious legal matters. We have represented individuals, families, businesses, and institutions across Kisii and beyond — with over 3,500 cases handled since establishment.

We craft bespoke legal strategies tailored to each client's unique circumstances. We believe that access to quality legal representation is not a privilege — it is a right.

What We Do

Our areas of practice

Comprehensive legal services across seven areas — delivered with analytical precision and an unwavering commitment to your best outcome.

01

Civil Litigation & Running Down

Strategic representation in civil disputes including motor vehicle accidents, personal injury claims, and general civil suits before Magistrates' and High Courts across Kenya. We fight tenaciously to secure the compensation and outcomes our clients deserve.

  • Motor Vehicle Accident Claims
  • Personal Injury & Damages
  • General Civil Suits
  • Debt Recovery & Enforcement
Motor ClaimsPersonal InjuryCivil Suits
02

Family Law

Compassionate yet rigorous guidance through all family legal matters — including divorce and separation, child custody, guardianship, adoption proceedings, and matrimonial property division — with sensitivity and expertise at every stage.

  • Divorce & Separation
  • Child Custody & Guardianship
  • Adoption Proceedings
  • Matrimonial Property Division
DivorceCustodyAdoption
03

Succession & Probate

Protecting your legacy through expert drafting of wills and trusts, letters of administration, succession certificates, and full probate administration — guiding families through one of life's most sensitive legal territories with care and precision.

  • Drafting Wills & Trusts
  • Probate Administration
  • Succession Certificates
  • Letters of Administration
WillsTrustsProbate
04

Property & Conveyancing

Expert guidance on all property transactions — conveyancing, land title disputes, lease agreements, and environmental compliance. We have extensive experience before the Environment and Land Court of Kenya and handle complex land matters with authority.

  • Conveyancing & Title Transfer
  • Land Title Disputes
  • Lease Agreements
  • Environment & Land Court
ConveyancingLand DisputesLeases
05

Employment Law

Protecting workplace rights for both employers and employees. We advise on contracts, unfair dismissal, redundancy, disciplinary proceedings, and represent clients before the Employment & Labour Relations Court with precision and authority.

  • Employment Contracts
  • Unfair Dismissal & Redundancy
  • Workplace Disputes
  • Labour Tribunal Representation
ContractsTribunalsRedundancy
06

Corporate & Commercial Law

Strategic legal counsel for businesses — from company formation and commercial contracts to corporate governance, joint ventures, and regulatory compliance. We serve startups and established businesses alike across East Africa.

  • Company Formation & Registration
  • Commercial Contracts
  • Corporate Governance
  • Regulatory Compliance
Company FormationContractsCompliance
07

Public Interest Litigation

Championing constitutional rights and fundamental freedoms through judicial review, human rights petitions, and public interest suits that serve justice beyond the individual client — upholding the rule of law for the benefit of all Kenyans.

  • Judicial Review Applications
  • Human Rights Petitions
  • Administrative Law
  • Constitutional Litigation

An Advocate of the High Court of Kenya, he established the firm in 2005 with a clear vision: to provide principled, expert legal representation to the people of Kisii and beyond.

His deep knowledge of Kenyan law — particularly in civil litigation, property, and employment matters — has secured exceptional outcomes for thousands of clients.
