The Firm

Built on trust & expertise

Founded in Kisii, Ochoki & Co. Advocates has grown from a boutique practice into one of Nyanza Province's most respected legal institutions. Under the founding leadership of Mr. Wilkins Ochoki, the firm established a reputation defined by analytical precision and strategic acumen.

Our attorneys are admitted Advocates of the High Court of Kenya, with extensive experience across both contentious and non-contentious legal matters. We have represented individuals, families, businesses, and institutions across Kisii and beyond.

We craft bespoke legal strategies that transcend conventional paradigms — harmonizing intellectual rigour with pragmatic solutions tailored to each client's unique circumstances. We believe that access to quality legal representation is not a privilege — it is a right.

What Sets Us Apart
  • Deep roots in the Kisii community and Kenyan legal system
  • A client-first philosophy — your priorities become our mission
  • Full-service capabilities from a single trusted firm
  • Transparent, honest communication throughout every matter
  • A track record of exceptional outcomes across all practice areas
  • Strong relationships with judges and courts across Nyanza Province

What We Do

Our areas of practice

Comprehensive legal services across nine areas — delivered with analytical precision and an unwavering commitment to your best outcome.

01

Employment Law

We advise employers and employees on all aspects of the employment relationship — contract drafting, workplace policies, disciplinary proceedings, redundancy, and representation before the Employment and Labour Relations Court. We work to ensure fair treatment and lawful outcomes for all parties.

  • Employment Contracts
  • Unfair Dismissal
  • Workplace Harassment
  • Labour Tribunal Representation
ContractsTribunalsRedundancy
02

Dispute Resolution

Our litigation team brings analytical precision and strategic thinking to every dispute. We represent clients through mediation, arbitration, and contested court proceedings — always seeking the most efficient and effective path to resolution without compromising on outcomes.

  • Commercial Litigation
  • Mediation & Arbitration
  • Debt Recovery
  • Injunctions
LitigationMediationArbitration
03

Estate Planning & Probate

Protecting your legacy requires careful planning. We assist with wills, trusts, letters of administration, succession certificates, and the full probate process — guiding you through one of life's most sensitive legal territories with compassion and clarity.

  • Drafting Wills & Trusts
  • Probate Administration
  • Succession Certificates
  • Letters of Administration
WillsTrustsProbate
04

Family Law

Family legal matters require sensitivity as much as expertise. We assist clients with divorce and separation, child custody and guardianship, adoption, matrimonial property division, and domestic violence protection orders — always prioritizing the wellbeing of families and children.

  • Divorce & Separation
  • Child Custody & Guardianship
  • Adoption Proceedings
  • Matrimonial Property
DivorceCustodyAdoption
05

Criminal Defense

When the stakes are highest, you need an advocate who will fight uncompromisingly in your corner. Our criminal defense team has extensive experience in the Magistrate's Court, High Court, and Court of Appeal — handling bail applications, full trials, and appeals with thoroughness and tenacity.

  • Bail Applications
  • Criminal Trials
  • Appeals & Review
  • Plea Negotiations
BailTrialsAppeals
06

Property & Real Estate Law

We navigate complex property transactions with expert guidance on conveyancing, land title disputes, lease agreements, and environmental compliance. We have extensive experience before the Environment and Land Court of Kenya.

  • Conveyancing
  • Land Title Disputes
  • Lease Agreements
  • Environmental Compliance
ConveyancingLandLeases
07

Corporate & Commercial Law

Empowering business growth with strategic legal counsel. We handle company formation, corporate governance, commercial contracts, joint ventures, intellectual property, and regulatory compliance — serving startups and established businesses alike across East Africa.

  • Company Formation
  • Commercial Contracts
  • Corporate Governance
  • Regulatory Compliance
ContractsGovernanceCompliance
08

Constitutional & Human Rights

Championing fundamental rights with strategic advocacy and constitutional law expertise. We bring judicial review applications, challenge unlawful administrative action, and represent clients whose constitutional rights have been violated by state or private actors.

  • Judicial Review
  • Human Rights Petitions
  • Administrative Law
  • Public Interest Litigation
Judicial ReviewRights
09

Immigration Law

Navigating complex global legal challenges with expert cross-border guidance. We assist with work permits, residency applications, citizenship matters, and immigration appeals for individuals and businesses operating across international borders.

  • Work Permits
  • Residency Applications
  • Citizenship Matters
  • Immigration Appeals
